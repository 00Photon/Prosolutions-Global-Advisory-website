Accra, Kigali, and Nairobi continue to deliver resilient yields for institutional landlords, especially in Grade A
mixed-use developments. Diaspora investment clubs are prioritising assets with embedded sustainability credentials and
anchor tenants from the technology or financial services sectors.

Europe tells a different story: liquidity remains tight, but trophy assets in Lisbon, Madrid, and Dubai are trading at a
discount compared to 2022 peaks. By partnering with indigenous asset managers and using data-led due diligence, our
clients are negotiating favourable vendor financing and expediting regulatory approvals.
